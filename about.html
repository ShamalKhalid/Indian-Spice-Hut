Spice Odyssey

KERALA RECIPES

Embark on a culinary journey through the flavors of Kerala with our traditional recipes at Indian Spice Hut. Experience the coastal magic with our Kerala Fish Curry, a tangy blend of tamarind and coconut milk. For vegetarians, our Avial offers a delightful mix of locally sourced vegetables in a coconut-yogurt gravy. Indulge in the aromatic delight of our Malabar Biryani, a fragrant rice dish layered with meat or vegetables. Don't miss our Kerala Parotta paired with Chicken Curry, a perfect fusion of flaky bread and robustly spiced chicken. Each dish at Indian Spice Hut is a tribute to Kerala's culinary heritage, inviting you to savor the authentic tastes and aromas that define this vibrant region. Join us for a delightful journey through the rich flavors of India.
